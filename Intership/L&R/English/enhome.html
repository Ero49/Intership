With more than 10 activities, English Bento makes the English learning process gamified
and engaged. English Bento is an interactive language learning app for students and a
score tracking admin for teachers.

The heart of Common Test English L&R Practice are the
activities and each trains a different English language skill.

Listen and Say

pronunciation and shadowing.
